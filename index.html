Where precision meets quality

GrandMoor is a third-party logistics (3PL) provider built for businesses that need their goods stored, managed, and moved without friction. Whether you're scaling an e-commerce operation or need a dependable partner for industrial distribution, GrandMoor is built to keep your supply chain moving.

Strategically Located

Our Mississauga facility offers direct highway access to the GTA, Southwestern Ontario, and cross-border routes, cutting transit time to your customers and suppliers.

Hands-On Partnership

GrandMoor isn't a faceless logistics operator. Our founding team is directly involved in day-to-day operations, sales, and account management, so you always have a real point of contact who understands your business.

Built to Scale With You

From single-pallet storage to full-facility dedicated space, our operation is structured to grow alongside your volume — without forcing you into rigid, one-size-fits-all contracts.

Operational Transparency

Clear reporting, defined SLAs, and a team that treats your inventory like it's our own. No black-box logistics — you know what's happening with your goods at every stage.

Warehousing and Distribution and E-commerce Order Fulfillment

Wherever your goods are in the supply chain, we have a service built for that stage.

WAREHOUSING & DISTRIBUTION

3PL Warehousing

Flexible, scalable storage inside our 16,000 square foot Mississauga facility. Our team handles receiving, putaway, and ongoing inventory oversight so your stock stays organized and easy to retrieve — without you needing to run a warehouse of your own.

  • Palletized and shelf storage
  • Inventory tracking and reporting
  • End-to-end receiving and putaway
  • Climate-conscious storage areas

Contract Warehousing

For businesses that need dedicated space without the burden of a long-term commercial lease. Contract warehousing terms flex with your volume, so you're not locked into space you don't need.

  • Flexible short-term and long-term storage agreements
  • Scalable space as your volume grows or contracts
  • Dedicated account oversight

Cross-Docking

For freight that needs to move through rather than sit in storage, our facility supports cross-docking — transferring goods from inbound to outbound with minimal handling time, reducing both cost and dwell time.

  • Inbound-to-outbound transfer coordination
  • Reduced dwell time for time-sensitive freight
  • Coordination with your carriers or ours

E-COMMERCE ORDER FULFILLMENT

Order Fulfillment

From the moment an order comes in to the moment it leaves our dock, our team handles picking, packing, and dispatch with accuracy — so your customers get the right product, on time.

  • Order picking and packing
  • Custom packaging options
  • Carrier coordination for outbound shipments
  • Same-day or next-day dispatch targets

Inventory Management

Real-time visibility into what's on your shelves. Our team maintains accurate stock counts and reporting cadences so you're never guessing about what you have or where it is.

  • Cycle counts and stock audits
  • Inventory reporting on a schedule that fits your business
  • SKU-level tracking
  • Low-stock and reorder alerts

Container Destuffing & Services

Efficient unloading of inbound containers, with goods sorted, inspected, and staged for storage or onward distribution — reducing handling time and demurrage costs.

  • Kitting and light assembly
  • Labeling and re-labeling
  • Quality inspection on receipt
  • Custom reporting for client-specific needs
