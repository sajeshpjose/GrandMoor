Our Service Pillars

Three core pillars: warehousing and distribution, e-commerce order fulfillment, and final mile delivery support

Complete Logistics Solutions

Grandmoor organizes its services around three core pillars to serve every stage of your supply chain. Wherever your goods are, we have a service built for that stage.

Pillar 1: Warehousing & Distribution

3PL Warehousing

Flexible, scalable storage with palletized and shelf storage options, inventory tracking, receiving, and putaway services.

Contract Warehousing

Flexible short-term and long-term agreements with scalable space and dedicated account oversight.

Cross-Docking

Inbound-to-outbound transfer coordination with minimal handling time and reduced dwell time.

Container Destuffing

Efficient container unloading with goods sorted, inspected, and staged for storage or distribution.

Pillar 2: E-Commerce Order Fulfillment

Order Fulfillment

Picking, packing, and dispatch with accuracy for same-day or next-day delivery to your customers.

Inventory Management

Real-time visibility with cycle counts, audits, and SKU-level tracking on your schedule.

Platform & WMS Integrations

Connect your storefront directly to our warehouse management system for real-time data sync.

Value-Added Services

Kitting, labeling, quality inspection, and custom reporting tailored to your needs.

Pillar 3: Final Mile & Distribution Support

Final Mile Delivery

Getting your product the last few kilometres to your customer's door, reliably and on schedule.

Shipment Tracking & Visibility

Stay informed on where your shipments are at every stage with real-time status updates.

Distribution & Carrier Coordination

Coordinate with your preferred carriers for LTL and full truckload transportation solutions.

Delivery Scheduling

Flexible scheduling support coordinated with your timeline and budget requirements.
