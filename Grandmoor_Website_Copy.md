## Notes Before Publishing

A few things worth deciding before this goes live:

1. **Confirm exact address.** Grandmoor Inc. is confirmed as the legal entity name and Mississauga as the facility city — you still need the street address and postal code filled in before publishing the Contact page and any map embed.
2. **Service accuracy.** A few items above (climate-controlled storage, kitting, same-day dispatch, response-time SLAs) are placeholder claims common in this industry. Confirm each before publishing so the site doesn't promise something operations can't deliver yet.
3. **Anchor client / case studies.** Once your anchor client relationship is formalized, a short "who we serve" or testimonial section could strengthen the Home and Offerings pages considerably.
4. **Positioning against larger competitors.** Established Mississauga-area 3PLs (e.g. QRC Logistics) compete on decades of history, in-house fleets, and broad multi-city coverage. As a new, single-facility operator, Grandmoor's stronger pitch is responsiveness and direct founder involvement rather than scale — the copy above leans into that, but it's worth keeping that contrast in mind as you add testimonials or case studies later.
5. **Offerings page scope.** The services page is now structured in three pillars (Warehousing & Distribution, E-Commerce Fulfillment, Final Mile & Distribution Support), modeled on how established Toronto-area 3PLs organize their sites. Several entries are marked "confirm before publishing" — container destuffing, platform/WMS integrations, final mile delivery, and shipment tracking all depend on capabilities Grandmoor may not have running yet. Better to launch with a shorter, accurate list than an impressive one you can't deliver on.
